

Series
    Has:
        1       User, owning
        0..*    Occurence(s)
        0..1    Series master
    Can:
        be exported as iCal
        be shared as read-only
        be shared as cloneable when it is the master

Occasion
    Has:
        1       Start and end
        1       Series
        0..1    Occasion master
        0..1    Forum
    Can:
        have widgets like forum attached
